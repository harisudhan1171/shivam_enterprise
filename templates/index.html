Frequently Asked Questions ?

1. What type of wood do you use for your doors?
We use high-quality seasoned hardwoods such as teak, sal, and country wood, chosen for their strength, natural beauty, and resistance to wear. Each wood type is carefully selected based on the customer’s needs and budget.
2. How do you ensure durability and resistance to termites or moisture?
Our doors and Frames are treated with industry-grade anti-termite solutions. We also use seasoned wood that has been properly dried to prevent warping, cracking, or fungal growth. Protective coatings and sealants further enhance longevity.
3. Do you offer custom sizes or designs?
Yes, we specialize in custom solutions. Whether you need a unique size, carved patterns, or specific finishes, we’ll craft doors to match your exact requirements
4. What are the standard sizes you offer?
Doors
  • Main doors: 3 ft x 6 ¾ ft
  • Room doors: 2 ¾ ft x 6 ¾ ft
  • Bathroom doors: 2 ½ ft x 6 ½ ft
Frames
  • Main doors Frame: 5 ft x 7 ft, 3½ ft x 7 ft
  • Room doors Frame: 3 ft x 7 ft, 3 ¼ ft x 7 ft
Custom dimensions are available on request.
5. Are there any budget-friendly options without compromising quality?
Absolutely. We offer a range of affordable doors made from durable country wood that maintain quality and aesthetics while staying within budget.
6. Do you offer discounts for bulk orders or repeat customers?
Yes, we provide attractive discounts for bulk purchases and returning customers. We value long-term relationships and offer loyalty benefits
7. Do you provide installation services?
Yes, we offer professional installation services within the local area. Our team ensures proper fitting and finishing for a hassle-free experience
